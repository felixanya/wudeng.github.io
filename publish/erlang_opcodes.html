BEAM Instruction Set

  • 参考文档

allocate t t Allocates Arg0 slots on the stack. Arg1 is Live.

allocate_heap t I t Allocates Arg0 slots on the stack. See allocate t t. Ensures that heap contains at least Arg1 words after HTOP. Arg2 is Live.

deallocate I Restores CP to the value referenced by EP. Adds Arg0 + 1 to EP to remove the saved CP and Arg0 slots from the stack.
